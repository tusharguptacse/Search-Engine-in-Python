The Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority (or SLSEA) is the primary body responsible for the issuance of licenses to sustainable energy developments in Sri Lanka. In addition to being the key licence provider, it is also in charge of promoting renewable energy and sustainable developments in the country.[1]
